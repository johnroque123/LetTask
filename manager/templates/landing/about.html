About

Built by one person.
For everyone.

LetTask is a personal productivity app designed and built by Aemard Roque — a student who got tired of juggling five different apps just to stay organised.

The story

It started with a problem every student knows — tasks in one app, schedules in another, notes scattered across sticky pads. No single free tool did it all.

LetTask started as a Django first practice project with one constraint: it had to be genuinely useful, not just a CRUD demo. Real auth with OTP, brute-force protection, AI assistant, habit tracker, sticky notes, draggable dashboard — all in one place.

What started as a practice project turned into something worth shipping.

"I didn't want to build another to-do list. I wanted the app I wish existed when I want to build something."

— Aemard Roque

What's inside

Six modules, one login

🔐

Auth System

OTP email verification, brute-force lockout, forgot/reset password, avatar upload.

✅

Tasks & Calendar

Full CRUD with priority and category. Monthly calendar with day modal.

🗓

Schedules

Timed events with start/end times, repeat rules, and timeline view.

📝

Notes

Colour-coded sticky notes with pin, search, and AJAX mutations.

🏆

Habits

7-day grid, 12-week heatmap, streak calculation — all AJAX toggled.

📊

Dashboard

Draggable, hideable widgets with layout persisted in localStorage.
